Foodie is concept app designed with the focus of quick and healthy recipes. Style is focused on earthy palettes and easy to navigate UI.

Show full project
Stages of design

To get to the design I am showing here, I first had to work out everything that didn't work. I decided on a main page wireframe layout, after which I decided on visual style, color, font, and such. Having these two figured out first was essential to get a good UI design. After that it was only a matter of putting them together.
