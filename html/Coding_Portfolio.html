About the designer

The content on this page is created by Frederik DeVilder, an detail-focused designer who loves to come up with optimized experience flows and crystal clear visual designs to guide user to their goals and objectives. He also regularly delves into the abstract world of coding, improving his understanding and skills of front-end development, such as OOP, HTML/CSS/JS, and frameworks.
